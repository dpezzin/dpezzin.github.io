Harvard University OpenCourseWare

This is a free series of online computer science courses offered by Harvard University. The lectures are all recorded live on Harvard's campus and uploaded to the course websites. It makes the material much easier to learn, because you can pause lectures to take notes.


Computer Science 1

Understanding Computers and the Internet

I recommend CS1 as an introductory course for anyone looking to improve their computer skills. No prior experience is required to take this course.

Website: http://computerscience1.tv/

Computer Science 50

Introduction to Programming

CS50 is great for anyone who wants to learn programming, but is not sure where to start. No prior programming experience is required, however it is assumed you are comfortable with the material covered in CS1.

Website: http://cs50.tv/

Computer Science 75

Building Dynamic Websites

CS75 picks up where CS50 left off and begins to get more advanced.

Website: http://cs75.tv/

Computer Science 76

Building Mobile Applications

CS76 is an introduction to Android and iPhone development.

Website: http://cs76.tv/

App Inventor

Free Tool

App inventor is a fantastic development tool for beginners. There is no code writing. Controls are all drag and drop. App Inventor projects can use native features like GPS and messaging. You can also download projects that other people have built.

Website: http://beta.appinventor.mit.edu/
